# Service Accounts not Marked “Sensitive and Cannot be Delegated”

## Description
Lists service accounts without NOT_DELEGATED set.

## Severity
MEDIUM

## Category
Service Accounts

## Remediation
For high-value service accounts, enable “Account is sensitive and cannot be delegated”.
